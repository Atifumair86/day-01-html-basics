Biryani-recipe

A perfect dish for every occasion

Description

Biryani is a popular and flavorful rice dish made with basmati rice, spices, and meat or vegetables, known for its rich aroma and layered cooking method. It is believed to have originated during the time of the Mughal Empire and later became especially famous in cities like Hyderabad. Today, many regional varieties of biryani are enjoyed across India and around the world.

Prep time - 1 hour

Cook time 1 hours

Total time 2 hours

Total servings 2 servings

Ingrideints

  • 500 g chicken
  • 2 onions (thinly sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chillies
  • ½ cup curd (yogurt)
  • Whole spices (2 cloves, 1 cardamom, small cinnamon)
  • Mint and coriander leaves
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder

Instruction

  1. Step 1: Wash and Soak Rice
  2. Step 2: Cook the Rice (70% only)
  3. Prepare Chicken Masala
  4. Layer the Biryani
  5. Dum Cooking
  6. Serve

chef's tips

  1. Use long grain basmati rice.
  2. Do not overcook rice.
  3. Cook on low flame during dum
